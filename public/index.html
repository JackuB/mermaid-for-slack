Mermaid Preview

Create Mermaid diagrams and charts in Slack with a /mermaid command.

How it works?

The app opens a modal on receiving a `/mermaid` command where users can input their Mermaid diagram code. Once submitted, the app generates a PNG and posts it as a message.

Use Mermaid diagrams to describe your ideas in Slack
